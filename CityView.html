Top activities to do in Bogota

Go visit Monserrate

This place give an amazing view of all the city.

Attend a Museum

You can find a great variety of museums that are close to each other.

Do a picnic in Simon Bolivar Park

Simon Bolivar Park is a perfect place for picnic, outdoor activities or just chilling out.

Enjoy night activities

This city have an amazing nightlife. You could go party, to pubs, among other activities.

Security tips

  • Hold your personal belondings close to avoid pickpockets.
  • Avoid going to dangerous neighborhoods.
  • Keep a low profile.
  • Avoid using street ATMs, instead use the ones inside malls.
  • At bars, don't drink something you didn't buy.
  • Don't walk around with bundles of cash.
  • Have a dummy wallet to hand over in case of a robbery.
  • At night it's preferable to trabel in a cab or Uber.
